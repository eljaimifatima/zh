Bio

Dr Zakaria CHAROUH

Received an M.Sc. degree from the ENSA Kenitra (UIT university), and PhD degree from EMI (Med V University). It was his privilege to teach computer science at several Moroccan universities, such as the International University of Rabat and the Moroccan School of Engineering Sciences, as well as at several international institutes, such as the French M2I-Formation institute and the Greek ZPeople institute. As a Research Scientist, he worked on several research projects funded by NARSA, the Moroccan Ministry of Transportation, and the Moroccan region of RSK, that involve the use of AI, Computer vision, and EVs on which he has published several research papers and patents. As R&D Manager, he is currently working on several projects implementing a Smart-City strategy in the Province of Berkane, in conjunction with the Ministry of Energetic Transition, the National Water and Forest Agency of Morocco, and other Moroccan institutes.
